# Ollama — Overview (OpenAI‑Compatible & Native)

- **OpenAI compatibility:** Experimental and subject to breaking changes; intended for drop‑in with the OpenAI Chat/Responses‑style clients.
- **Tool calling:** Added in 2024; **streaming tool calls** landed May 28, 2025. Behavior is evolving—validate on your installed version.
- **Compatible endpoints:** Community notes indicate the compatibility layer primarily supports `/v1/chat/completions` today (Requests for `/v1/responses` support are tracked).
